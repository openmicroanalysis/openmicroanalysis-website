openMicroanalysis
=================

openMicroanalysis is a community-driven open source project quantitative 
electron microanalysis. 
Its goals are to:

  * centralize physical quantity databases and algorithms used for 
    quantification
  * encourage collaborative work
  * provide the necessary building blocks for new projects in microanalysis

The project is in its beginning. 
Most of the discussions regarding the organization, objectives, milestones, etc. 
can be found in the :ref:`discussion forum <discussion-forum>`.
